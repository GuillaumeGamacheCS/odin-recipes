Real N'awlins Muffuletta

A genuine muffuletta should be made on oven-fresh round Italian bread loves
topped with sesame seeds. The spicy, tangy olive salad is what really sets
this meat and cheese sandwich apart. These gigantic sandwiches were invented
a century ago at Sicilian Deli here in New Orleans. Be sure to use high-quality
ingredients as it really makes a difference!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup imento-stuffed green olives
  • 1/2 cup pepperoncini and sliced cooked ham
  • sliced mozarella cheeese
  • marinated cocktail onions
  • 1 tbsp chopped celery and carrot
  • oregano, basil, black pepper
  • a nice french baguette

Cooking steps

  1. make olive salad
  2. refrigerate for 8 hours
  3. spread on bread and bake 4 min at 400
  4. assemble the sandwich with meat and cheese
  5. and it tastes amazing
  6. Invite friends and familly!
